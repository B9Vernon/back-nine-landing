Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How much does it cost to play?
Bay rentals are $60/hour plus 5% tax. Up to 3 guests are included — no extra charge. All courses and games are included in the hourly rate.
Do I need to be an experienced golfer?
Not at all. Back Nine is designed for all skill levels — from complete beginners to competitive players. We have beginner-friendly modes, short course options, and games everyone can enjoy.
How long does a round of 18 holes take?
On average, 18 holes takes 45 minutes to an hour solo. For groups, expect 1–2 hours depending on the number of players. You can also play 9 holes or do practice sessions within your booked time.
What is a membership and how does it work?
Members pay a flat monthly fee for unlimited access during their membership hours. No hourly charges, no booking fees — just show up and play. Members also get priority booking and complimentary guest access.
Can I host a corporate event or private party?
Absolutely. We host corporate events, team-building sessions, tournaments, birthday parties, and more. Fill out our event inquiry form or call us at 236-361-7888 for a custom quote.
Do I need to bring my own clubs?
You're welcome to bring your own clubs but it's not required. Loaner clubs are available — just let us know when you book.
Where are you located?
